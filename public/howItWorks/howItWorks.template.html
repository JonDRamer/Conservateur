How It Works

The first step to building your collection is to fill out our consultation form. The cost of booking a consultation is $19.99. What do you get for that price? A professional curator will review your information and reach out to you with some additional questions. After they have gathered all of your requirements they will search our extensive network of artists for pieces that meet your criteria. Next your curator will send you an email with 6 paintings that match your personal style and budget.

Keep an eye out for an email from your curator. Review each personalized recommendation from your curator. Once you have selected that perfect piece for your space your curator will send you an invoice.

That's it! Your painting will arrive in no time and you will be hanging that perfect new addition to your space!
